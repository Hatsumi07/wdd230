The Name of my Site:

Comas Chamber Commerce Site

The Purpose of my Site:

The purpose of the site is to support Comas’ local businesses and promote their services or products. It will also help Comas’ citizens to be more aware and involved on the main issues that impede Comas from developing. The site will inform Comas’ citizens about volunteering events, health campaigns, touristic places in our city, social events, and any other topics that can contribute to our city’s growth.
